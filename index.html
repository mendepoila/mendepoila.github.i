Facts about Karaganda:

1.The name "Karaganda" is derived from "caragana" bushes, which are abundant in the area.

2.A worldwide known boxer Gennadiy Golovkin was born and raised in one of the regions in Karaganda.

3.Karaganda is a city of a coal mining industry. It generates the biggest part of coal produced in Kazakhstan.
